Hello! My name is Nathan Kidanemariam and I'm a senior at Saint Anselm's Abbey School in Washington D.C. I live for programming and creating things such as an Ardunio Shield Bot and Smart Glasses. This year I've attained my HTML Certification after 300 hours of coursework and I've also gotten my Google Ads certification for business adverisement strategies. Outside of research, I love playing soccer (emphasis on the love), and I also enjoy tennis, baskeball, playing the saxophone, debating, and creative writing.

Programming- I first felt called to programming when I saw my father working on numerous projects from work such as PLC programming and escalator repair. While I didn't understand things at that time, I realized that I wanted to be part of slow but steady technological advancement of our era. To this extent, I worked with my dad to understand the basic concepts of HTML, Java, and C++ while also learning ladder logic, a PLC language. On my own, I took courses and exams online to learn more, which is why I've been able to add Python as one of my new Langauges. I've also been doing different projects for outside reinforcement.
